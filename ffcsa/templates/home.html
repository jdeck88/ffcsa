Our Livestock

Grassfed Cattle

Our cows are raised and finished the way nature intended – on 100% grass, herbs, and clover. During the winter months, our cattle are fed balage, a method for fermenting sprint-time forage which provides a high quality diet and consistent rates of gain throughout the year. This results in a beef product that has more beneficial fatty acids (from the 100% forage based diet), is higher in flavor, and maintains tenderness.

Pasture-Raised Pigs

We allow our hogs to root around in open pastures and oak woodlands during the Spring, Summer, and Fall and feed them a diet of our custom-milled dairdadgrains and hazelnuts. Their well-rounded diet, exercise, and access to pasture results in a high quality, flavorful meat that consistently draws praise from people up and down the Willamette Valley.

Spring Lamb

Our lambs are raised and rotated on pastures throughout the year. We will occasionally graze our lambs on other organic land. These are either dedicated pastures or cover crops on other organic farms.

Pastured Chickens & Turkeys

Our pasture-raised broilers are raised on pasture where they consume up to 20% of their diet on grass, herbs, and clover. The birds are moved daily to fresh forage, keeping them well-fed and happy. We also raise Bourban, Bronze, and White Turkeys. All birds are raised on pasture and housed at night to prevent predation.

Laying Hens

Our Certified Organic by Oregon Tilth laying hens are housed in our custom-built mobile hen houses. They are moved twice/ week 10 months out of the year on pasture. As our layers are allowed the freedom to roam outside and forage on grass and eat bugs, the eggs are high in Omega-3’s. Laying hens also play a vital role in our pasture management as they contribute to the fertility of our soils through their manure, as well as help break up parasite life cycles for our other livestock species like cattle, sheep, and pigs.

Raising Animals Simply, to Simply Raise the Bar

We aspire to create a regenerative model that improves the planet and does not deplete resources like other industrial mono cropped agriculture systems.

We are a resource to the aspiring generations of young farmers.

We make no compromises in practicing what we believe to be the necessary future of farming.

We actively engage in conservation initiatives on the land.

We support our local food community.

We take a long-term approach with our sustainable livestock operation.

We pursue ongoing improvement to the health of the land on and around our farm.

We do not use herbicides, pesticides, growth hormones, sub-therapeutic antibiotics or synthetic nitrogen fertilizers.

We generate soil fertility through the natural cycles of composting, grazing management, and seeding a variety of forages.

We are 100% transparent with what our animals eat.

We regenerate the soil with composted manure and minerals which replenishes the nutrients needed to grow lush forage.

Benefit From Our Long-Term Approach

At Deck Family Farm, we know you want to feed your household delicious food that was responsibly and locally sourced. So you need a farm focused on bringing you nutrient-dense meat, milk, and eggs while making our planet healthier.

The problem is that meat and egg production standards are lacking, leaving you craving a better option. You deserve to know that your food was raised a better way.

Like you, we want meat from sustainably raised and humanely treated animals, raised in harmony with nature. That’s why our multigenerational farm is deeply committed to raising the bar for production standards and holds the highest certification.

You can Help Raise the Bar
By shopping at Deck Family Farm you support your local food community and enable us to be a resource to aspiring generations for young farmers. You can be confident that our goal is to make no compromises in practicing and teaching what we believe to be necessary to reclaim a farming economy.

At Deck Family Farm, you’ll benefit from our long-term approach in developing a sustainable livestock operation. By seeking to improve the health of the land, we pass on to you a greater nutrient-dense bounty working in closer tandem with nature.

With your health in mind, the farm does not use herbicides, pesticides, growth hormones, sub-therapeutic antibiotics, or synthetic nitrogen fertilizers. Instead, we generate soil fertility through the natural cycles of composting, grazing management, and seeding a variety of grasses. We know you want a farm that is creating a regenerative model that doesn’t harm the planet in ways that are associated with industrial, mono-cropped systems of agriculture.

So stop questioning if your meat and eggs were produced responsibly, and feel confident your farmer is putting the health of both you and the planet first.
