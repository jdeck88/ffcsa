Pastured Chickens & Turkeys

+

Our pasture-raised broilers are raised on pasture where they consume up to 20% of their diet on grass, herbs, and clover. The birds are moved daily to fresh forage, keeping them well-fed and happy.

We also raise Bourban, Bronze, and White Turkeys. All birds are raised on pasture and housed at night to prevent predation.
